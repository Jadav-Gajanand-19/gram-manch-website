Frequently Asked Questions

Is GramManch really free?

Yes! GramManch is completely free to download and use. No hidden charges, no premium features. It's built for community empowerment.

Can I use it without knowing how to read?

Absolutely! GramManch is voice-first. Just speak your commands and the app will respond. No typing or reading required.

Is my identity safe when reporting issues?

Yes. Our anonymous reporting feature keeps your identity completely private. Only your voice is heard, not your name.

What if my village doesn't have internet all the time?

GramManch works offline! You can browse resources and prepare reports offline. They'll sync when you're back online.

How do I rent farm equipment?

Go to AgriRent, browse available tools, select dates, and book. Pay directly to the owner. Simple and transparent!

Can men use this app too?

While designed for women's empowerment, the entire community benefits. Men can view announcements and resources.
